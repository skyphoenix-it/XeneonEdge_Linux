What you get

Everything a side screen should do — and nothing it shouldn't.

Built as a real Linux citizen: a Rust core, a Qt6 UI, and a desktop companion that makes styling it effortless.

Plug in, it just appears

Detects the Xeneon Edge by its EDID and lands fullscreen on the right screen — surviving hotplug, rotation, and reboots. No display-picker roulette.

22 widgets, swipeable pages

System gauges, clock & calendar, weather & moon, a Pomodoro focus timer, tasks, habits, hydration, now-playing media, countdowns and more — arranged your way.

Style it without config files

A desktop Manager app shows a live WYSIWYG clone of your Edge. Drag to reorder, resize, retheme, and drop in wallpapers — changes push to the device instantly.

22 themes & living backdrops

Nord, Dracula, Gruvbox, Synthwave, Catppuccin and more, with frosted-glass cards over animated aurora, waves, starfield, mesh, or bokeh. 14 accents on top.

Featherweight & private

~3.5% CPU worst-case, near-zero when idle, ~378 MB steady. No telemetry, no account, no cloud — everything stays on your machine.

Engineered to stay up

A Rust core and Qt6 front-end, single-instance-safe, with 95%+ automated test coverage across four layers — including tests that drive the real binary end-to-end.
